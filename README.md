# Open Datasets

This repository contains a curated selection of teaching datasets originally sourced from the UK Data Service and made available under an open-access licence.
The data is provided here to support interactive, online exploration and learning activities.

**British Social Attitudes Survey, 2021, Health Care and Equalities: Open Access Teaching Dataset**
Location in this repository: https://github.com/JenniferLB/Open_data/blob/master/bsa2021_healthcare_equalities_open.dta
Citation and link to access from the UK Data Service: 
University of Manchester, Cathie Marsh Institute for Social Research (CMIST), UK Data Service. (2024). British Social Attitudes Survey, 2021, Health Care and Equalities: Open Access Teaching Dataset. [data collection]. NatCen Social Research, [original data producer(s)]. UK Data Service. SN: 9236, DOI: http://doi.org/10.5255/UKDA-SN-9236-1
